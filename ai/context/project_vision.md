# Project Vision

## Project Name

EasyFeedback

---

## Problem Statement

After interviews, interviewers spend a lot of time writing up structured feedback.
The goal is to let them provide only their rough notes (a few lines) plus the interview
transcript, and have the app generate polished, ready-to-use feedback against a chosen
template — cutting the write-up from a long manual task to a couple of minutes.

Who experiences it: interviewers across hiring teams who must produce consistent,
template-conformant feedback after every interview.

---

## Users

- **Interviewers** — provide notes + transcript, receive generated feedback.
- (Likely later) **Hiring managers / recruiters** — consume the feedback and the
  pass / no-pass / talent-pool recommendation.

---

## Core Features

- Select an available feedback template, or load / create a new one.
- Provide the interview transcript plus a few lines of notes.
- Generate feedback that conforms to the chosen template, ready to copy.
- Optionally accept the role requirements / summary and produce a decision
  recommendation: **pass**, **no pass**, or **keep in talent pool**.

---

## Constraints

- No hard constraints identified yet (TBD as the stack and infra firm up).

---

## Success Criteria

- Interviewers can obtain reliable, template-conformant feedback within a couple of
  minutes of finishing an interview.
- Output is consistent and trustworthy enough to use with minimal manual editing.
