# Personas

## Persona 1

### Name

Interviewer

### Goals

- Produce structured, template-conformant feedback right after an interview.
- Capture the decision recommendation (pass / no pass / talent pool) with little effort.
- Spend under a minute on the write-up instead of a long manual task.

### Pain Points

- Writing feedback manually is time-consuming, so it gets deferred to "later."
- By the time they come back to it, important details are forgotten, making the
  write-up harder and the result less reliable.

### Main Workflows

- Select (or create / load) a feedback template.
- Paste the interview transcript plus a few lines of notes.
- Generate feedback ready to copy.
- Optionally supply the role requirements to get a pass / no-pass / talent-pool
  recommendation.
